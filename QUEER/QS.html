Queer Terminology surrounding Sexualities and Romance

Sexual terms

Homosexual

The Term "Homosexual" refers to people who have an exclusive sexual attraction to genders alike their own. Due to the very clinical qualities of this term, the terms "gay" for the masculine and "Lesbian" for the feminine are used almost exclusively, with some regarding as hostile to do otherwise.

Homoflexible

The term "Homoflexible" refers to people who have a sexual or romantic attraction to genders alike their own, but may occasinally exhibit interest in genders unalike their own.

Bisexual

The term "Bisexual" refers to people who have a sexual attraction to genders alike their own and genders unalike their own. A similar term is "Polyseuxal", but these two categories do not like being labeled as the other.

Pansexual

The term "Pansexual" refers to people who have sexual attraction that does not meaningfully deliniate between genders. A similar term is "Omnisexual", but these two categories do not like being labeled as the other. May be shortended to "Pan" in speech or writing.

Heteroflexible

The term "Heteroflexible" refers to people who have a sexual or romantic attraction to genders unalike their own, but may occasinally exhibit interest in genders alike their own.

Romance terms

Homoromantic

The Term "Homoromantic" refers to people who have an exclusive romantic attraction to genders alike their own. The terms "Achillean" for the masculine and "Sapphic" for the feminine are more likely to be used.

Biromantic

The term "Biromantic" refers to people who have a romantic attraction to genders alike their own and genders unalike their own. A similar term is "Polyromantic", but these two categories do not like being labeled as the other. May be shortended to "Bi" in speech or writing.

Panromantic

The term "Panromantic" refers to people who have romantic attraction that does not meaningfully deliniate between genders. A similar term is "Omniromantic", but these two categories do not like being labeled as the other. May be shortended to "Pan" in speech or writing.

Polyamorous

The term "Polyamorous" refers to people who pursue relations with multiple people simeltaneously.

Demisexual, Demiromantic

The term "Demisexual" refers to people who have not interst in sex without first the establishment of an emotional attraction.The term "Demiromantic" refers to people who have no interst in romance without first the establishment of an emotional attraction. While this may be a typical progression of interest for most couples, with a "Demi" as it may be abridged in speech, an emotional attraction is a requirement.
